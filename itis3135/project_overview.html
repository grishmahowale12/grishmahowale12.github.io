Client Project Overview

Project Overview:

  • The application will be a website for the South Asian Student Association (SASA) at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. The primary purpose of this website is to serve as a comprehensive online platform for promoting and celebrating South Asian culture, traditions, and heritage. The website aims to unite South Asians and individuals from other cultures through educational and cultural events, offering a space to learn about the diversity and richness of South Asia.
  • The website will be accessible to SASA members and other students at UNC Charlotte who are interested in South Asian culture, language, and traditions. It will serve as a hub for promoting upcoming events, activities, and initiatives, fostering engagement and collaboration within the South Asian community.
  • The website will include information about SASA's mission, its Executive Board, its membership, and its activities. It will feature an events page for upcoming events such as cultural celebrations, language classes, workshops, and social gatherings, alongside a membership page detailing the benefits of joining SASA and how to get involved.

Site Map:

Page design:

  • Index page: The index page will introduce SASA, its purpose, and its mission. It will provide an overview of the organization and its goals.
  • Executive Board page: This page will feature profiles and introductions of the Executive Board members, including their roles within the organization.
  • Events page: This page will showcase upcoming cultural events, workshops, movie nights, and other activities hosted by SASA.
  • Membership page: This page will provide details on membership eligibility, benefits, and how to join SASA.
  • Contact page: The contact page will include contact information, social media links, and a form for prospective members and the public to get in touch with SASA.

Dynamic Functionality on the website:

The dynamic functionality that I plan on implementing using JavaScript includes:

1. Forms for the Membership page to allow users to register and inquire about joining SASA.

2. Interactive image sliders on the Executive Board and Index pages to showcase images from past events and activities.

3. A calendar feature to display upcoming events and important dates for SASA activities.
