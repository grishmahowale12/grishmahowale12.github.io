What is Dance?

Dance is a universal language that transcends words, allowing people to express emotions, tell stories, and connect with others through movement. At its core, dance combines rhythm, music, and the body to create a unique form of art that has been part of human culture for centuries. Whether performed as a solo, in pairs, or in groups, dance has the power to convey joy, sorrow, excitement, and even a sense of mystery.
