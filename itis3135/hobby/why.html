Why Dance Matters

Dance uplifts the spirit, offers a way to connect with others, and promotes physical and mental well-being. It encourages empathy and understanding as people explore different styles and forms from around the world. It can reduce stress, boost mood, and even improve physical fitness by building strength, flexibility, and coordination.
