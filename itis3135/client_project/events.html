Holi 2K24

Holi is called the festival of colors, spring, and love. It is the most popular and significant Hindu holiday that marks the beginning of Spring. The festival is symbolic of the triumph of good over evil and has been celebrated since the 4th century CE. We will be providing colors free of charge. We are honored to have DJ Maks of Maks Entertainment providing live DJ services for the event. This event is funded in part by the Student Government Association with your student activity fees.

SASA x SKY Yoga and Meditation

A spin-off of our monthly Yoga Event, we are collaborating with SASA (South Asian Student Association) for a February Yoga Event! We will have certified SKY instructors leading some group yoga exercises indoors. Yoga mats will be provided, but supply is limited. This is an opportunity to have a peaceful evening and to meet and get to know new people with similar interests!
