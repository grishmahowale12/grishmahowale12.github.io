About SASA

The South Asian Student Association (SASA) is an organization committed to sharing and uplifting the diverse cultures, religions, languages, and traditions of South Asia. Through our vast membership, we create a welcoming, fun-loving, and purposeful community of students on campus.

By participating in this organization, you will gain the following career skills:

  • Communication
  • Engagement Across Perspectives
